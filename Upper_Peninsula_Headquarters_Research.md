# Upper Peninsula Michigan Headquarters Research

## Summary
Initial research on Upper Peninsula Michigan properties for headquarters reveals limited direct commercial real estate listings but provides historical and geographical context. 

## Findings
1. **Geographical Context**
   - The Upper Peninsula (UP) borders Lake Michigan to the west and Lake Superior to the east.
   - Key locations include Sault Ste. Marie, Calumet, and Marquette.

2. **Historical Properties**
   - Sault Ste. Marie Historic Commercial District shows the area's strategic importance.
   - Mackinac Island and Fort are notable historic sites.

3. **Infrastructure**
   - WEC Energy Group provides utilities in the UP.
   - Limited direct commercial listings found in initial search.
